About

If you are reading this, then you wanted take a second to understand me better in order to see if I could indeed help. Everyone is good at something. Some people are builders, some managers of people, some make beautiful art. As for me, I have always been an educator. If you were to watch my family’s home movies you would in no time find me explaining things in great detail to the camera.

Helping others to understand has always been a passion of mine and something that I find natural and fun. This is not to say that I am the best or that can explain anything to anyone. Rather it is to give you an idea of who I am and why I care. We each have something to offer and this is mine. I take something other people know and make it something you know. If someone else can do it, I am sure you and yours can too.

No one works magic, not even magicians. Once its shown clearly how the trick is done, it stops being mystical and starts becoming mundane. The same applies to anything in the realm of learning, especially when it comes to forms of mathematics. Once explained, it is shown for what it truly is: small simple steps with majestic results. Now... let’s pull a rabbit out of your hat!

Do you live with ADD/ADHD?

If you are reading this, than you are quite special. You or someone you love has one the greatest blessings/curses that we find our world today- being born with ADD. At some times, achieving more than anyone expected, while at others falling short of even acceptable. Your struggle is not unique, even though you are. The CDC now says that upwards of 20% of our population has some form of ADD. In my opinion this shows that it is a completely misnamed occurrence. To call anything that effects one fifth of our population a “disorder” is absurd. You are not broken, our system is. Sadly, while we are in the process of fixing that, true change will not be seen for years. In the meantime, figuring out how to dance to the music played by society, even if you are double-time to the music, is still something that should be practiced. As someone who has only known a world through the lens of ADD I offer my condolences and help.

The world has so many things to thank the ADD community for, whether it be Michelangelo’s inventive work or Einsteins reality changing theorems you had better believe ADD was one of the driving forces. This didn’t stop with them and it wont stop with you. ADD people will be at the forefront of changes today and in the future. However, figuring out how to work with our special skill is of the utmost importance. George Washington Carver would have effected none of us if he hadn’t figured out how to take proper notes. Marie Currie wouldn’t have saved thousands of lives if she never figured out how to organize her thoughts and results. Working with the system is how anything gets done.

This is not to say that there is something wrong with the way you do things, rather this is to say world is made of many types and to get true work done it takes accounting for all of them. While we may have be built the world it doesn’t mean the world is built for us. Together however, we will change the world and for the better.

Thank you for reading.
